Wikibook

Course: Core Typography

This is a project on how to present articles on the Web as a physical book.Familiarity with the grid and the layout of text and images is the focus of this project.I hope that I can connect the theme of the article with the overall impression given by the layout without affecting the legibility, so that the content of the article can be better understood.The wikipedia I chose was Marine life, so I chose cyan as the main color, and hoped to show the flowing of water through the layout.
